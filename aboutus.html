Little About Us

We started RoboLectro STEAM Studio to follow our passion

Passionate

Two Engineers who worked in the corporate world, quit their jobs to pursue their passion of imparting knowledge and teaching students. (A match that was lit when it benefitted their own son).

Latest Courses with STEM based curriculum

Kids learn STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths) through courses that solely focus on practical and hands-on training. Concepts are explained by giving real world examples. Varied courses like LEGO Robotics, Electronics, Coding and Communication Skills are furnished for students of the age group of 5 years -20 years.

LEGO certified trainers

Kids will be taught by trained by LEGO certified trainers from Carnegie Mellon University, PA, USA.

Outreach Programs

These programs involve frequent interactive sessions with experts across different domains (IISc, ISRO and many more)that would ultimately help the students in developing the skills of innovation and discovery.

Mentoring for competitions

Kids who are passionate and focused will be mentored for globally acclaimed robotics and science competitions like FLL, WRO, Robocup, Google CodeToLearn, etc. Our teams have won more than 15 awards during the past 2 years.

Represented India and won awards in Robotics Competition

Our students have represented India and won the Best Technical Skills Award in the Robocup Asia Pacific International competition held in Moscow, Russia (Nov 2019) and will also be participating in Japan (Oct 2020).

Welcome To RoboLectro STEM Studio


The RoboLectro STEM Studio is a place where kids explore Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Math through fun hands-on experience based activities. We offer After-school programs, Weekend workshops, Workshops in Apartments, Summer camps, Winter camps, Vacation camps, STEM-based birthday parties and Corporate events.

Choose Your Classes

We offer varied courses for students aged from 5- 20 years. The 5 main verticals of courses are LEGO Robotics, Science Fun Club, Electronics, Coding and Communication Skills. The curriculum of each course is age appropriate.
